Welcome to Raw to Runway

Welcome to RAW TO RUNWAY, the most anticipated fashion extravaganza in the heart of India. Established in 2017, Delhi Runway Week has rapidly ascended to become a cornerstone event in the global fashion calendar, attracting designers, models, fashion enthusiasts, and industry experts from around the world.

Our Mission
At RAW TO RUNWAY, our mission is to celebrate and promote the diversity and creativity of fashion. We aim to provide a dynamic platform for emerging and established designers to showcase their talent, fostering innovation and setting trends that influence the global fashion landscape. We are committed to excellence, inclusivity, and sustainability, ensuring that every show not only dazzles the audience but also respects and reflects the evolving values of our society.

What We Do
Fashion Shows: We host a series of stunning runway shows featuring collections from top-tier designers, rising stars, and international fashion houses. Each show is meticulously curated to highlight the unique vision and artistry of the designers, providing them with an unparalleled platform to present their latest creations.

Exhibitions and Pop-Ups: Our event includes exclusive exhibitions and pop-up shops, offering attendees the opportunity to experience and purchase cutting-edge fashion and accessories firsthand. These spaces also allow designers to interact directly with their audience, fostering a deeper connection between creators and consumers.

Workshops and Panels: Education and dialogue are key components of RAW TO RUNWAY. We organize workshops and panel discussions with industry leaders, covering topics such as sustainable fashion, innovative design techniques, and the future of the fashion industry. These sessions provide valuable insights and inspiration for both aspiring designers and fashion enthusiasts.

Networking Opportunities: RAW TO RUNWAY is a hub for networking, bringing together a diverse array of professionals from the fashion industry. From designers and models to buyers and media, our event facilitates meaningful connections and collaborations that drive the industry forward.

Our Impact
RAW TO RUNWAY is more than just a fashion event; it’s a cultural phenomenon that reflects the vibrant spirit of Delhi and the dynamic nature of the global fashion industry. We are proud to have launched the careers of numerous designers and models, many of whom have gone on to achieve international acclaim. Our commitment to sustainability and ethical fashion practices has also set new standards within the industry, inspiring change and promoting a more responsible approach to fashion.
